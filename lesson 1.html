HOW TO USE SKETCH UP


Cabinet Design Plan


Steps of Cabinet Making in Sketch up

Step 1: Press “R” and click the center of the axis (origin), then slightly to right.
Step 2: type “50,60” enter.
Step 3: Click at any corner of the rectangle and press “R” measures 60 for the height, repeat the process to the other sides making a whole cabinet.
Step 4: Press “T” and click the midpoint then type “1” enter, repeat the process on all sides.
Step 5: Press “T” and click the upper right corner then type “30”.
Step 6: Press “R” and click the lower left corner of the cabinet and type “23,6” enter, same as on the lower right corner of the cabinet.
Step 7: Press “T” and click the midpoint then type “1” enter.
Step 8: Repeat the process in step 6 and step 7 to make 8 drawers of the cabinet.
Step 9: Press “P” then click any drawer and pull outward then type “17”
Step 10: Press “T” then type “1” enter, repeat the process on all sides of the drawer.
Step 11: Press “R” and click the corner going to the opposite corner.
Step 12: Press “P” and move downward then type “5” and make a rectangle at the back part of the drawer
Step 13: Press “Space Bar” key then click the small rectangle inside the drawer, then press “Delete” key.
Step 14: Press “P” then type “1”
Step 15: To make another opened drawer just repeat the step 9 to step step 14 and just change the measurement if you want to.
Step 16: Press “E” to erase the unnecessary guidelines
Step 17: Press “P” and click the big rectangle above the drawers of the cabinet and move inward then type “17”
Step 18: Click the “Paint bucket tool” and choose the wood color dark brown.
For the handle, just get it from the google.
