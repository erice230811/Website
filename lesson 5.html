Fish Tocino Recipe

How to cook Fish Tocino


Ingredients:

Tuna Fish
Sugar
Salt
Anisado Wine
Pineapple Juice
Water

Procedure:

1. Mix all sugar and salt.
2. Coat the meat with the mixture
3. Add all the liquid ingredients
4. Marinade for 30 minutes.
5. Fry it in low heat.
