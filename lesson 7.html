Bonsai Tree

The ultimate goal of growing a Bonsai is to create a miniaturized but realistic representation of nature in the form of a tree.

What is bonsai?

an ornamental tree or shrub grown in a pot and artificially prevented from reaching its normal size.

Can you turn any tree into a bonsai?

Almost any tree or shrub can be turned into a bonsai.

Is it easy to make bonsai?

some basics can be learned quite easily
